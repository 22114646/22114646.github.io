Unit 1 - The Online World

Unit 1 and Unit 2 were the two theory units of the Level 2 IT course, and all the learning and small tests was done in KnowItAllNinja.

Unit 1 also supports the learning and understanding of Unit 8 and Unit 13 as well.

Learning Aim: A

Investigate online services and online communication.

I had to learn about the different types online services such as e-commerce, education, communication etc...

Other important topics were understanding online document systems and how/why online commnications is be used.

Learning Aim: B

Investigate components of the internet and how digital devices exchange and store information.

I learnt the difference between the World Wide Web and the internet, as well as their structuring and functions.

I had to understand the processes and purpose of Email, and the concepts, processes and implications of both Data Exchange and Data Storage.

Learning Aim: C

Investigate issues with operating online.

I had to learn about the possible threats to data and the steps to take to prevent them.

Unit 2 - Technolodgy Systems

Learning Aim: A

Understand how the components of technology systems work together.

I had to:

  • Understand the concept of a computer and technology system.
  • Understand the application of technology systems used in different sectors.
  • Understand the features, uses and implications of hardware devices.
  • Understand the concepts, applications and implications of networks.
  • Understand the concepts, implications and processes of data transfer.

Learning Aim: B

Understand how data flows between internal components of a computer and is processed to provide information.

I had to learn about the internal components of a computer and their functions and roles. I also needed to learn about the concept, process and implications of data transmisstion.

Learning Aim: C

Understand different types of software.
