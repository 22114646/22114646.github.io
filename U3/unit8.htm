Unit 8 - Mobile Apps Development

In this unit was mainly about how to design, develop, test and review a mobile app. Skills obtained from other units can be used

Learning Aim: A

Understand the characteristics and uses of mobile apps.

I had to review 2 different mobile apps then discuss the strengths and weaknesses of the app and how the features affects the usability of the app.

Learning Aim: B

Design a mobile app.

The task given to me was to design a alphabet app to help children aged 3-7 years old recognise the shapes and sounds of the alphabet.

Learning Aim: C

Develop and test a mobile app.

I had developed a few mobile app designs set as tasks, for practice and understanding. I also had to develop the alphabet app and documented changes made to the original design.

Learning Aim: D

Review the finished mobile app.

I had test and review the app, also justifying the changes/refinements made to the design.
