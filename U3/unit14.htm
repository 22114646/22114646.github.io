Unit 14 - Installing and Maintaining Computer Hardware

Learning Aim: A

Understand the benefits and implications of installing and maintaining hardware in technolodgy systems.

I had to do an investigation about the benefits and implications (possible issues) with installing and maintaining hardware.

Learning Aim: B

Plan installation and maintenance of hardware in a technology system.

I had to create a plan for installing 2 computer components as well as diagnosing and fixing faults within the system.

Learning Aim: C

Installing, maintain and test hardware in a technology system.

I had to follow the plan i created to install the 2 computer components, diagnose and fix the faults which were time not being kept on the system and the computer not booting up. I also had to upgrade the RAM, install a PCIe USB 3.0 card and a graphics tablet to meet client requirments.

Learning Aim: D

Review the modified technology system.

After installing and maintaining the system I had to review the modified system to the original plan and justify any changes made. I also had to recommend further improvements and consider constraints I encountered.
